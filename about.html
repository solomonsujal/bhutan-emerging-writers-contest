About the Contest

The Bhutan Emerging Writers Poetry Contest 2026 is a national initiative created to encourage creativity, discover new literary voices, and celebrate the rich culture and imagination of Bhutanese writers.

The contest welcomes emerging poets from every Dzongkhag of Bhutan. Whether you are a student, a working professional, or simply someone who loves writing poetry, this competition provides an opportunity to share your work with a wider audience.

Our Mission

  • Promote creative writing in Bhutan.
  • Encourage young and emerging poets.
  • Recognise outstanding literary talent.
  • Create opportunities for publication and recognition.
  • Build a stronger literary community in Bhutan.

Vision

To become one of Bhutan's most respected annual poetry competitions and inspire future generations of Bhutanese writers.
