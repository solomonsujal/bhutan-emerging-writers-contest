Welcome!

Welcome to the official website of the Bhutan Emerging Writers Poetry Contest 2026. This national poetry competition aims to discover talented Bhutanese poets and provide them with a platform to share their creativity with the nation.

Contest Highlights

  • 🇧🇹 Open to all Bhutanese citizens.
  • ✍ One original poem per participant.
  • 📄 Maximum 200 words.
  • 💰 Entry Fee: Nu. 50
  • 🏆 Total Prize Pool: Nu. 20,000
  • 📅 Submission Deadline: 28 August 2026
  • 📢 Winners Announcement: 15 September 2026
