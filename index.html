Welcome to the Bhutan Emerging Writers Poetry Contest 2026

The Bhutan Emerging Writers Poetry Contest 2026 is an independent national poetry competition created to encourage emerging Bhutanese writers and celebrate original creative expression.

Contest Information

  • Eligibility: Open to all Bhutanese citizens.
  • Entry Fee: Nu. 50
  • Maximum: One original poem (up to 200 words).
  • Submission Deadline: 28 August 2026
  • Total Prize Pool: Nu. 4,000
  • Winners: Three winners will be selected.

Prize Distribution

🥇 First Prize — Nu. 2,000

🥈 Second Prize — Nu. 1,500

🥉 Third Prize — Nu. 500

Ready to Participate?

Read the contest rules carefully, complete the payment, and submit your poem before the deadline.
