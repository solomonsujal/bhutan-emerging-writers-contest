Welcome

Welcome to the official website of the Bhutan Emerging Writers Poetry Contest 2026. This independent national poetry competition celebrates original voices and encourages emerging Bhutanese writers to share their creativity.

Contest Information

  • Eligibility: Open to all Bhutanese citizens.
  • Entry Fee: Nu. 50
  • Maximum Length: One original poem (up to 200 words).
  • Submission Deadline: 28 August 2026
  • Total Prize Pool: Nu. 4,000

Prize Distribution

🥇 First Prize — Nu. 2,000

🥈 Second Prize — Nu. 1,500

🥉 Third Prize — Nu. 500

Ready to Participate?

Read the contest rules, complete the payment, and submit your poem before the deadline.
