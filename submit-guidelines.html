Submission Guidelines

Please Read Before Submitting

  • Only one original poem is allowed.
  • Maximum length: 200 words.
  • Poems must be written by the participant.
  • AI-generated or AI-edited poems are prohibited.
  • Previously published poems are not accepted.
  • Upload your poem in PDF or DOCX format.
  • Keep a copy of your poem for your records.
