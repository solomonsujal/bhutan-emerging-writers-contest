Contest Rules

  1. Only Bhutanese citizens are eligible to participate.
  2. Each participant may submit only one original poem.
  3. The poem must be written entirely by the participant.
  4. AI-generated or AI-assisted poems are not permitted.
  5. The maximum poem length is 200 words.
  6. Poems must be submitted in PDF or DOCX format.
  7. The entry fee is Nu. 50.
  8. Payment proof must be uploaded with the submission.
  9. Submissions received after the deadline will not be accepted.
  10. The judges' decision will be final.

Important Dates

Submission Opens: 1 August 2026

Submission Closes: 28 August 2026

Results Announcement: 15 September 2026

Disqualification

Entries containing plagiarism, offensive content, false information, or any violation of these rules may be disqualified without notice.
