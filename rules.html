Contest Rules

Bhutan Emerging Writers Poetry Contest 2026

  • Open to all Bhutanese citizens.
  • Entry Fee: Nu. 50.
  • Only one poem per participant.
  • Maximum 200 words.
  • AI-generated or AI-edited poems are not allowed.
  • Submission Period: 1 August 2026 – 28 August 2026.
  • The judges' decision is final.
