Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ

Who can participate?

Any Bhutanese citizen may participate.

How many poems can I submit?

Only one poem per participant.

What is the entry fee?

Nu. 50 per entry.

Can I use AI?

No. AI-generated or AI-edited poems are not accepted.

When are the results announced?

The results will be announced after judging is completed.
